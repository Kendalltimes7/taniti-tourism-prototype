Travel Information for Visitors

Visitor FAQ

This page includes practical information visitors may want before traveling to Taniti.

Contact Information

Phone: (555) 555-5555

Email: info@visittaniti.com

Hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

Currency

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many businesses also accept euros and yen.

Power Outlets

Power outlets are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

Safety Information

Violent crime is very rare, although petty crime such as pickpocketing may occur.

Hospitals and Medical Services

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Drinking Age

The drinking age on Taniti is 18.
